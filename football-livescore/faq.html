FAQs

1. How many leagues or teams can I subscribe at the same time?
- Unlimited as your wish.
- You can view available leagues in the "Explore" screen.
- You can view available teams in the "Team" tab.
- We suggest to utilize the search functions so that you can find the suitable one.
- To subscribe: Long press on the item to open options/actions views or use subscribe button in detail screen.
- To unsubscribe: Long press on the item to open options/actions views or use subscribe button in detail screen or use "Notification Subcriptions" screen in "More" tab.

2. What about delay between notification and real match?
- It is totally up to multiple factors from our networks, your network and data availability.
- You will receive it as soon as data is available in our system, we do not have exact number but ideal situation within 1 minute
- Notification will be discarded after 300s if server is not able to deliver message to your phone

3. Overwhelmed with too much notification?
- First strategy is to personalize the notification scenarios as your need: score, match begin, match end, first haft end, second haft begind, extra time.
- Consider to subscribe to your favourited teams first, before subscribe to the whole leagues.
- You can also subscribe at the match level however we do not recommend this as you will need to manual unsubscribe it after the match completed
- We also provide you the option to turn off notifications while still retain all configurations.

4. How to synchronize premium purchases with my different devices? Or What if I change to new device?
- We provide option to restore purchases on "More" tab.
- Once click on it, your previous purchases will be restored for your devices.
